Start a Colony in Three Steps

We're here to support beginners every step of the way. Ant keeping is safe, ethical, and endlessly fascinating when done right. Whether you're a curious hobbyist, educator, or nature enthusiast, we'll guide you through the essentials with care and clarity.

01

Choose the Right Queen (Inquiry)

Start by selecting a queen ant suited to your region and experience level. We'll help you understand local legality and seasonal availability, ensuring you get a healthy queen ready to establish her colony.

02

Set Up the Habitat

Create a comfortable home with proper hydration and escape prevention. We provide complete DIY ant farm kits and guidance on humidity, nesting chambers, and foraging areas—everything your colony needs to thrive.

03

Feed, Observe, Learn

Establish a simple weekly routine: provide protein and sugars, monitor activity, and watch your colony grow. Ant keeping is a rewarding, low-maintenance hobby that reveals nature's intricate social structures up close.

New to this? We'll help you pick a setup that matches your space and comfort level. Reach out anytime—we're here to guide you.
